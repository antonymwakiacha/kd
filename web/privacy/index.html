Privacy Policy

Overview

Kenyan Dictionary is an informational app that provides users with access to Kenyan dictionary words and idioms. The app does not take any input from users; it only displays dictionary content for viewing purposes.

Offline Storage

Dictionary data is cached locally on your device using Hive to allow offline access. No personal information is stored on the device.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Supabase solely to fetch dictionary content. No personal data is collected, stored, or shared.

Disclaimer

All dictionary entries displayed in this app are sourced from the official Kenyan Dictionary Instagram page and are used for informational purposes only. The app does not claim ownership of this content. For official definitions or updates, please refer to the original Kenyan Dictionary Instagram page.
